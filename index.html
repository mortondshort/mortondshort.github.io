What is Heroes of the Storm?

Here is a short explanation about what Heroes of the Storm is

Heroes of the Storm is a relatively new multiplayer online battle arena which has 5 players on each team fight against eachother and become the first team to destroy the enemies core.

Heroes of the Storm has 9 different maps which are different shapes and hold different objectives, the main objective for every game is to kill the enemies core wether is be by having your team run up and manually kill the core, or have giants or even pirate cannons kill the core for you, but that's easier said than done.

In Heroes of the Storm you can either queue up with up to 5 friends in a quick match, or play in Hero League which is the more competitive mode for solo or duo players, you then earn a ranking for how well you play the game.

Key Features in Heroes of the Storm

Heroes of the Storm is a very unique Multiplayer online battle arena game which has multiple features which aren't in any other moba.

Team Level

Unlike all other mobas, Heroes of the Storm has a joint level for your team, most over mobas has levels for each character.

Talent System

In Heroes of the Storm you pick a talent at levels 1, 4, 7, 10, 13, 16 and 20. At level 10 you pick a heroic ability out of 2 options.

Objective Based

In Heroes of the Storm there are different objectives for each map, controlling/capturing or killing these objectives can give you large advantages in the game and can even the end the match.

Roles

There are 4 roles in Heroes of the Storm

Assasin

Damage Dealer

Assasins are usually either mage characters or heroes that mainly do damage from auto attacks, assasins are key to kill the enemy team and kill the minions..

Warrior

High Health Heroes

Warriors, also known as tanks are heroes that have high health pools that can run in to team fights and soak up damage, they also engage team fights with their high amount of lock ups.

Support

Healers and Utility

Supports are heroes that can heal their team mates, they also often have a lot of utility that helps keep their team alive, for example shields and cleanse.

Specialist

Siege and Specialisation

Specialist heroes are very unique heroes that often have traits and skills that help your team siege forts, they often do damage overtime instead of burst damage.
